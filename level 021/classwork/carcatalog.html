Audi A5 | BMW 3 Series | Range Rover

Audi A5

Engine: 268-hp turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder
Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic
Drivetrain: all-wheel drive

The new A5 has a single powertrain comprised of a turbocharged 268-hp 2.0-liter four-cylinder assisted by a 48-volt hybrid system. No manual transmission is available, as a dual-clutch automatic handles gear swaps for the A5 as well as its high-performance V-6-powered S5 sibling.

BMW 3 Series

Base Engine: Most models (like the 330i) come with a 2.0-liter TwinPower Turbo inline-4 engine.
Performance: This engine produces 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque.
Transmission: It is paired with an 8-speed Steptronic automatic transmission.

Optional Upgrade: For those seeking more power, there is also an available straight-six cylinder engine that delivers 386 horsepower.

Range Rover

3.0-Liter Inline-Six (Mild-Hybrid): This is a core engine for the Range Rover and Range Rover Sport. It typically utilizes a 48-volt mild-hybrid system to assist with efficiency and power delivery. Output levels vary by trim.
